---
title: Counterfactual
date: 2026-04-12T09:20:00Z
mode: science
---

A hypothesis is stronger when it names the world in which it would be unnecessary.
Counterfactuals expose whether a mechanism is doing work or merely borrowing atmosphere.
If the conclusion survives after removing the preferred story, the model may be portable.
If it collapses, the narrative was load-bearing.
